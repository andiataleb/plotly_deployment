Project Overview

Bacterial species that can synthesize a protein that tastes like beef. The Improbable Beef company has a hypothesis that this microorganism could be found in human belly button bacteria. There has been a group of volunteers whose navel bacteria were sampled and studied in this project. This dashboard can help both researchers and participants visualize the top 10 bacteria species of each volunteer using their assigned anonymous ID Number.
